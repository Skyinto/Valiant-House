Valiant House

Empowering displaced and unhoused individuals and families in Northern California.





Sometimes all you need is a little good soil.


Ask any gardener, and they will tell you one of the main components to a healthy plant is sunlight, water, and good soil.

We’ve seen the determination of people who, like plants, with courage and determination, can grow. It takes a lot of courage and willingness to start something new and different.

We’ve seen the plants struggling to push through the concrete, and we want to provide you with good soil. We want to provide you or your clients a safe and stable environment to grow and see what beautiful future can blossom.

We want to welcome you home, provide you support, encourage you, and give you a community so that you can thrive and blossom.




About Us


At Valiant House, we are proud to offer supportive housing in a safe environment. An on-campus apartment-style housing program allows our guests increased privacy, privileges, and responsibilities. There are [X] units for families or single individuals.

This includes a communal kitchen and a common living room for relaxing and socializing. Valiant House is a unique program that gives our guests a way to smoothly transition into permanent housing in thriving communities. This experience allows our guests to engage with the community in a positive way.









Who we Serve





  • Women and children

  • Veterans


  • Young Adults aging out of foster care


  • Unhoused and displaced individuals


  • Individuals seeking re-entry assistance














Our Core Values


At Valiant House, we pride ourselves on remaining true to our core values.


Compassion



We believe in being kind and caring to everyone we encounter.

Respect

Integrity

Dignity
